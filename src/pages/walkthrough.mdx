# Walkthrough

This is a complete walkthrough for Pokémon Emerald Imperium, covering everything from the truck to the Elite 4. If you prefer, you can also view the [Google Docs version](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1vTBsTHbHfUzU9OR8F4xs9V3ae1TcLhYLeOro3rcsGFg).


## Split 1 - Roxanne

<div className={styles['walkthrough-section']}>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Littleroot Town</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Go upstairs in the first house and set your clock.</li>
    <li>Go next door, upstairs, and talk to your rival.</li>
    <li>Go south to Professor Birch’s Lab and get your starter.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 103</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight your Rival on the northern side.</li>
    <li>Rival team listed under “Rivals” tab.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Littleroot Town</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Return to Birch’s Lab for your Pokédex.</li>
    <li>Head north to Oldale Town, then west to Petalburg City.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Petalburg City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Speak to the Fisherman at the northern lake for the Old Rod.</li>
    <li>Visit the Gym and watch Wally's catch tutorial.</li>
    <li>Go west to Petalburg Woods.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Petalburg Woods</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight the Team Aqua Grunt halfway through.</li>
    <li>Go north to Route 104.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 104</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight Dawn at the northern bridge.</li>
    <li>Continue north to Rustboro City, then east to Route 116.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 116</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight Roark at the far end.</li>
    <li>Return to Rustboro City.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Rustboro City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Defeat Roxanne in the Gym.</li>
    <li>Get HM Cut from the house southwest of the Poké Mart.</li>
    <li>Head east toward Rusturf Tunnel.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## Split 2 - Brawly

<div className={styles['walkthrough-section']}>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Rusturf Tunnel</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight the Team Aqua Grunt.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Rustboro City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight your Rival when exiting to the south.</li>
    <li>You can now optionally fight Gardenia in Petalburg Woods.</li>
    <li>(She has a stronger team after Wattson.)</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 104</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Talk to Mr. Briney to sail to Dewford Town.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Granite Cave</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Talk to the first Hiker to get HM Flash.</li>
    <li>Deliver the letter to Steven at the top floor.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Optional</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Return to Rustboro to get evolution stones from the Devon CEO.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Dewford Town</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Defeat Brawly in the Gym.</li>
    <li>Sail with Mr. Briney to Slateport City.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## Split 3 - Wattson

<div className={styles['walkthrough-section']}>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Slateport City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Talk to Dock (south of the museum).</li>
    <li>Re-enter the museum, get Flipturn from a grunt, and talk to Capt. Stern.</li>
    <li>Fight Team Aqua Grunts (back-to-back).</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 110</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight mandatory trainers and your Rival.</li>
    <li>Optional: Fight trainers in the Beach House to get a Charcadet.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Mauville City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Get Rock Smash, Stat Editor, Bike, Gift Pikachu, and the Good Rod.</li>
    <li>Optional: Fight Fantina on Route 117.</li>
    <li>Fight Wally in front of the Gym.</li>
    <li>Defeat Wattson in the Gym.</li>
    <li>Access the Game Corner.</li>
    <li>Exit west to Verdanturf Town via Route 117.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## Split 4 - Flannery

<div className={styles['walkthrough-section']}>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Verdanturf Town</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Use Rock Smash in Rusturf Tunnel to get HM Strength.</li>
    <li>Purchase evolution items if needed.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 111</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight Dawn and clear routes through Route 112 and Hiker Trent.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Fiery Path → Route 113</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Collect Lucario Egg in the house after Fiery Path.</li>
    <li>Defeat minibosses on Route 113 to reach Fallarbor Town.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 114</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Cross the snow bridge, then continue south to Meteor Falls.</li>
    <li>Cutscene with Team Magma.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Mt. Chimney</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Take the Cable Car from Route 111.</li>
    <li>Fight three bosses: Magma Double, Tabitha, and Maxie.</li>
    <li>Take the Meteorite from the pedestal.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Lavaridge Town</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Get Gift Eevee and TMs from the vendor outside the Poké Mart.</li>
    <li>Optional: Fight Maylene in Jagged Pass.</li>
    <li>Defeat Flannery in the Gym.</li>
    <li>Acquire the Mega Ring.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div className={styles['walkthrough-section']}>

## Split 7 - Tate & Liza

<div className={styles['walkthrough-section']}>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 121</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight mandatory rain battles.</li>
    <li>Optional: Fight Byron.</li>
    <li>Trigger a Team Aqua cutscene.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Mt. Pyre</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Go to the summit for a cutscene with Maxie.</li>
  </ul>

  <ul>
    <li>Trigger the Aqua event at the Harbor.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Lilycove City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Defeat Dawn.</li>
    <li>Enter Team Aqua's Hideout and defeat them.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 124 → Mossdeep City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Fight sun-based minibosses.</li>
    <li>Get the Super Rod.</li>
    <li>Defeat Tate & Liza in the Gym.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

## Split 8 - Juan

<div className={styles['walkthrough-section']}>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Mossdeep City</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Trigger Space Center cutscene.</li>
    <li>Go to Steven’s house to get HM Dive.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Lavaridge Town → Jagged Pass</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Discover and clear Team Magma’s Hideout.</li>
    <li>Final fight with Maxie.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Route 127 → Seafloor Cavern</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Use Dive on Route 128.</li>
    <li>Defeat Team Aqua in the cavern.</li>
  </ul>
  <div className={styles.locationHeading}>Sootopolis City → Cave of Origin → Sky Pillar</div>
  <ul>
    <li>Watch the cutscene and speak with Wallace.</li>
    <li>Free Rayquaza at Sky Pillar.</li>
    <li>Return to Sootopolis.</li>
    <li>Speak to Archie and Maxie.</li>
    <li>Get HM Waterfall from Wallace.</li>
    <li>Defeat Juan in the Gym.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
