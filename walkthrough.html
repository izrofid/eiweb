Walkthrough

This is a complete walkthrough for Pokémon Emerald Imperium, covering everything from the truck to the Elite 4. If you prefer, you can also view the Google Docs version.

Split 1 - Roxanne​

Littleroot Town
  • Go upstairs in the first house and set your clock.
  • Go next door, upstairs, and talk to your rival.
  • Go south to Professor Birch’s Lab and get your starter.
Route 103
  • Fight your Rival on the northern side.
  • Rival team listed under “Rivals” tab.
Littleroot Town
  • Return to Birch’s Lab for your Pokédex.
  • Head north to Oldale Town, then west to Petalburg City.
Petalburg City
  • Speak to the Fisherman at the northern lake for the Old Rod.
  • Visit the Gym and watch Wally's catch tutorial.
  • Go west to Petalburg Woods.
Petalburg Woods
  • Fight the Team Aqua Grunt halfway through.
  • Go north to Route 104.
Route 104
  • Fight Dawn at the northern bridge.
  • Continue north to Rustboro City, then east to Route 116.
Route 116
  • Fight Roark at the far end.
  • Return to Rustboro City.
Rustboro City
  • Defeat Roxanne in the Gym.
  • Get HM Cut from the house southwest of the Poké Mart.
  • Head east toward Rusturf Tunnel.

Split 2 - Brawly​

Rusturf Tunnel
  • Fight the Team Aqua Grunt.
Rustboro City
  • Fight your Rival when exiting to the south.
  • You can now optionally fight Gardenia in Petalburg Woods.
  • (She has a stronger team after Wattson.)
Route 104
  • Talk to Mr. Briney to sail to Dewford Town.
Granite Cave
  • Talk to the first Hiker to get HM Flash.
  • Deliver the letter to Steven at the top floor.
Optional
  • Return to Rustboro to get evolution stones from the Devon CEO.
Dewford Town
  • Defeat Brawly in the Gym.
  • Sail with Mr. Briney to Slateport City.

Split 3 - Wattson​

Slateport City
  • Talk to Dock (south of the museum).
  • Re-enter the museum, get Flipturn from a grunt, and talk to Capt. Stern.
  • Fight Team Aqua Grunts (back-to-back).
Route 110
  • Fight mandatory trainers and your Rival.
  • Optional: Fight trainers in the Beach House to get a Charcadet.
Mauville City
  • Get Rock Smash, Stat Editor, Bike, Gift Pikachu, and the Good Rod.
  • Optional: Fight Fantina on Route 117.
  • Fight Wally in front of the Gym.
  • Defeat Wattson in the Gym.
  • Access the Game Corner.
  • Exit west to Verdanturf Town via Route 117.

Split 4 - Flannery​

Verdanturf Town
  • Use Rock Smash in Rusturf Tunnel to get HM Strength.
  • Purchase evolution items if needed.
Route 111
  • Fight Dawn and clear routes through Route 112 and Hiker Trent.
Fiery Path → Route 113
  • Collect Lucario Egg in the house after Fiery Path.
  • Defeat minibosses on Route 113 to reach Fallarbor Town.
Route 114
  • Cross the snow bridge, then continue south to Meteor Falls.
  • Cutscene with Team Magma.
Mt. Chimney
  • Take the Cable Car from Route 111.
  • Fight three bosses: Magma Double, Tabitha, and Maxie.
  • Take the Meteorite from the pedestal.
Lavaridge Town
  • Get Gift Eevee and TMs from the vendor outside the Poké Mart.
  • Optional: Fight Maylene in Jagged Pass.
  • Defeat Flannery in the Gym.
  • Acquire the Mega Ring.

Split 5 - Norman​

Optional
  • Before or after Norman, explore the Route 111 Desert.
  • Fight minibosses and get HM Surf.
Route 110 → Petalburg City
  • Fight Wally near the Trick House.
  • Complete three floors of the Trick House.
Petalburg Gym
  • Defeat Norman.
Optional
  • Fight Wake on Route 118 east of Mauville.

Split 6 - Winona​

Route 118 → Route 119
  • Clear the Weather Institute.
  • Fight your Rival.
Fortree City → Route 120
  • Cross the wooden bridge and talk to Steven for the Devon Scope.
  • Return to Fortree and fight Winona.

Split 7 - Tate & Liza​

Route 121
  • Fight mandatory rain battles.
  • Optional: Fight Byron.
  • Trigger a Team Aqua cutscene.
Mt. Pyre
  • Go to the summit for a cutscene with Maxie.
Slateport City
  • Trigger the Aqua event at the Harbor.
Lilycove City
  • Defeat Dawn.
  • Enter Team Aqua's Hideout and defeat them.
Route 124 → Mossdeep City
  • Fight sun-based minibosses.
  • Get the Super Rod.
  • Defeat Tate & Liza in the Gym.

Split 8 - Juan​

Mossdeep City
  • Trigger Space Center cutscene.
  • Go to Steven’s house to get HM Dive.
Lavaridge Town → Jagged Pass
  • Discover and clear Team Magma’s Hideout.
  • Final fight with Maxie.
Route 127 → Seafloor Cavern
  • Use Dive on Route 128.
  • Defeat Team Aqua in the cavern.
Sootopolis City → Cave of Origin → Sky Pillar
  • Watch the cutscene and speak with Wallace.
  • Free Rayquaza at Sky Pillar.
  • Return to Sootopolis.
  • Speak to Archie and Maxie.
  • Get HM Waterfall from Wallace.
  • Defeat Juan in the Gym.

Split 9 - Elite 4​

Evergrande City
  • Fight your Rival and Dawn.
  • Optional: Fight Volkner.
Victory Road
  • Fight Wally.
  • Clear Victory Road.
  • Challenge the Elite 4 and Champion.
